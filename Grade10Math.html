Grade 10 Mathematics

Grade 10 Mathematics is a course that covers the following topics:

Term 1

Topics

  • Exponents, equations and inequalities

  • Solving linear and quadratic equations
  • Simultaneous equations
  • Inequalities
  • Trigonometry
    • Reciprocals
    • 2D problems

Topics

  • Euclidean Geometry
  • Analytical Geometry
  • Functions

  • Trigonometry (2D)

  • Finance and growth

Term 4

Topics
